Dog Catcher

  • High Concept: try to not get caught from the dogcatcher, while catching the dog bones, but watchout ...if the bone falls to the ground it will brake.
  • Player Experience Goals: I want the players to have the expierence that he is trying to catch bones while being afraid of the dog catcher.
  • Genre: a single player in third person.
  • Game play:the player can go left and right and it can jump up and down. As level goes up the levels become more diffucult
  • Prototype goal: the mechanic of basic ai, where the dog catcher has a mind of his own.
  • Features: the unique parts is the insentative of chathing more bones
  • Setting/Premise: thedog has ran away from dog pund, and now is being chase by one of the employees from the dog pound. Your job is to get away from and find a new home.
  • Story: Get the puppy away from the dogcatcher into a new home.
  • Target Audience: My demographic is people that want to spend sometime playing a few video games.
  • Play time: around 5 min give or take.
  • Strategies: being really good at timing how the dog catcher works.
  • Prototype Assets: I made everything from scratch, and I am very proud
